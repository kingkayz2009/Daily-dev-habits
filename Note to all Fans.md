# Project Schedule & Timeline

This schedule outlines the planned flow of activities and updates for this project.
The goal is steady progress through clearly defined days, not rushed execution.

---

## Day One – Project Foundation
On the first day, the focus will be on setting up the repository.
This includes creating essential files, defining the project purpose,
and establishing a clear structure to guide future work.

---

## Day Two – Content Planning
The second day will be dedicated to planning.
Ideas will be reviewed, refined, and organized to ensure the project direction
remains simple, practical, and achievable.

---

## Day Three – Initial Development
On this day, core content and materials will begin to take shape.
Small but meaningful contributions will be added to move the project forward.

---

## Day Four – Review and Adjustment
The fourth day will focus on reviewing progress made so far.
Improvements will be identified, unnecessary complexity removed,
and clarity prioritized.

---

## Day Five – Expansion
Additional features, prompts, or documentation will be introduced.
This stage allows room for creativity while staying aligned with the project goals.

---

## Day Six – Testing and Refinement
All existing content will be tested for usability and consistency.
Edits will be made where necessary to improve overall quality.

---

## Day Seven – Reflection and Update
The final day of the cycle will be used for reflection.
Lessons learned will be documented, progress evaluated,
and plans made for the next phase.

---

This schedule is flexible and may evolve as the project grows,
but consistency remains the core objective.
